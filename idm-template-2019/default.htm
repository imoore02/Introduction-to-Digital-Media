About the Project

This is your assignment template for Introduction to Digital Media which you will update throughout the course and will form the basis of your Assignment 3. Although the design of this website fairly uninspired, you will have a chance to modify the look and feel in Assignment 3. For the time being, we will focus purely on content and not design.

When you're ready, you should update the content on this page with information about your project using HTML techniques covered in Week 3.

Website Structure

  • Home: The page you're viewing now. You should update the content here to describe your project for assignment 1..
  • Creative Journal: This page contains the journal you started in Weeks 1 & 2.
  • Promo Video: You will embed the video you produce for Assignment 2 here.
  • Photo Gallery: This page will feature images related to your project.

You should update the content on Home and Creative Journal pages for Assignment 1. You will be provided instructions on how to do this in your tutorials.
